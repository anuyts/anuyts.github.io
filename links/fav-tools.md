## Some of my Favorite Tools

(This list is in progress.)

### Web Search
- [Marginalia Search](https://marginalia-search.com/): I'm not really using it yet, but I enormously appreciate the initiative.

### LaTeX
- [Detexify](https://detexify.kirelabs.org/classify.html): Convert scribblings to LaTeX symbols

### Citation management & bibliography
- [Online Bibtex Converter](https://asouqi.github.io/bibtex-converter/): Convert bibTeX to textual references.
- [doi2bib](https://www.doi2bib.org/): Convert DOI to bibTeX entry
- [Analog Library Premium Edition™](https://al.radbox.org/): Distraction-free DOI pages for ACM papers
